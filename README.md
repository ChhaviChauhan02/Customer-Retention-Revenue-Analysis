# 📊 Customer Retention & Revenue Intelligence Platform

## End-to-End Business Intelligence & Customer Analytics Project

This project demonstrates an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution developed using **Excel, SQL Server, Python, and Power BI** to analyze customer retention, subscription performance, revenue trends, and customer satisfaction.

The project follows a complete analytics workflow—from data preparation and validation to SQL-based business analysis and interactive Power BI dashboard development—providing business users with actionable insights for improving customer retention and revenue performance.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Subscription-based businesses need to continuously monitor customer retention and revenue performance to sustain long-term growth.

However, business teams often struggle to answer questions such as:

- Which subscription plans generate the highest revenue?
- How effectively are customers being retained?
- Which customer segments contribute the most business value?
- How satisfied are customers with the service?
- Which business metrics require immediate attention?

The objective of this project was to transform raw customer data into an interactive Business Intelligence dashboard that enables stakeholders to monitor customer behaviour, revenue performance, retention trends, and customer satisfaction through a single reporting platform.

---

# 📌 Project Objectives

The project was developed to:

- Analyse customer retention performance.
- Monitor revenue across subscription plans.
- Evaluate customer satisfaction trends.
- Identify high-value customer segments.
- Build an executive dashboard for business decision-making.
- Enable customer-level drill-through analysis.
- Demonstrate an end-to-end analytics workflow using SQL Server, Python, and Power BI.

# 📈 Dashboard Overview

The Power BI report is organized into six interactive pages, each designed to support different stages of customer and revenue analysis.

### 🏠 Home Page
Provides an introduction to the project and serves as the landing page with navigation to all dashboard sections.

### 📊 Dashboard Overview
Displays executive KPIs and a high-level summary of customer retention, revenue performance, and subscription metrics.

### 🔄 Customer Retention Dashboard
Analyzes customer retention performance, subscription behaviour, and retention trends across different customer groups.

### 💰 Revenue Dashboard
Examines revenue performance across subscription plans and customer segments to identify business growth opportunities.

### 👥 Customer Segmentation Dashboard
Segments customers based on key business attributes, enabling comparison of customer groups and their contribution to business performance.

### 🔍 Customer Details (Drill-through)
Provides customer-level analysis through Power BI drill-through functionality, allowing users to investigate individual customer records and supporting metrics in greater detail.
