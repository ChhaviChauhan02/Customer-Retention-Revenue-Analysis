# 📊 Customer Retention & Revenue Analysis

## End-to-End Business Intelligence Project

This project demonstrates an end-to-end customer analytics solution developed using **Excel, SQL Server, Python, and Power BI**.

The objective was to analyze customer retention, subscription behavior, revenue trends, and customer satisfaction to identify business opportunities for improving customer loyalty and revenue growth.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Subscription-based businesses often struggle with:

- Customer churn
- Declining retention
- Revenue leakage
- Poor customer satisfaction
- Limited visibility into customer behaviour

The goal of this project was to transform raw customer data into actionable business insights through an interactive analytics platform.
